# Risk Register – AIC

## 1. Structural Risks

| ID | Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|----|------|------------|--------|------------|
| R1 | Policy bypass attempt | Medium | High | Built-in immutability |
| R2 | Human override abuse | Medium | High | Multi-level escalation |
| R3 | Archive node loss | Low | Critical | Distributed replication |
| R4 | Simulation miscalculation | Medium | Medium | Counterfactual policy |
| R5 | Governance capture | Low | Critical | Amendment supermajority |

## 2. Ethical Risks

| ID | Risk | Description |
|----|------|-------------|
| E1 | Normalization of automation | Overreliance on AI |
| E2 | Responsibility diffusion | No clear accountable actor |
| E3 | Decision acceleration bias | Skipping delay policies |

## 3. Long-Term Risks

- Drift toward optimization-only culture
- Economic pressure to weaken safeguards
- Political pressure to centralize control
- Gradual erosion of human authority

---

## 4. Risk Review Cycle

Risk register must be reviewed:

- On every major release
- After every major failure
- During constitutional amendment proposals
